## 4. Go-to-Market Strategy (Africa)

### Why Africa, Why Now
- **Regulatory momentum:** Nigeria SRG1 (2026), SA Carbon Tax, Kenya Climate Act
- **IFRS adoption:** Multiple African countries adopting IFRS S1/S2
- **Fragmented landscape:** 54 countries, no unified tracking infrastructure
- **Donor dependency:** $50B+ annual aid flows need accountability
- **Carbon potential:** Africa holds 30% of global carbon credit potential
- **Digital leapfrog:** Mobile-first populations ready for digital verification

### Entry Markets (Phase 1)
| Country | Entry Point | Regulation Driver |
|---------|------------|-------------------|
| Nigeria | Corporate ESG + NGO reporting | SRG1 (2026), IFRS adoption roadmap |
| Kenya | Government SDG + carbon projects | Climate Change Act, carbon market |
| South Africa | Corporate ESG + carbon tax | Carbon Tax Act, JSE requirements |

### Expansion Markets (Phase 2)
| Country | Opportunity |
|---------|------------|
| Ghana | Carbon market development, AfCFTA |
| Rwanda | Digital governance leader, green growth |
| Egypt | COP host legacy, green hydrogen |
| Morocco | Renewable energy, EU proximity |

### Target Segments
1. **NGOs (Quick Win):** Structured donor reporting saves 40+ hours/quarter
2. **Corporates (Revenue):** IFRS S1/S2 compliance is non-negotiable by 2027
3. **Governments (Scale):** National SDG tracking across departments
4. **DFIs/Donors (Trust):** Verified data improves funding allocation

## 5. Revenue Model

### SaaS Subscriptions
| Tier | Target | Price Range | Key Features |
|------|--------|-------------|--------------|
| Lite (Free) | Citizens, small NGOs | $0 | Basic reporting, 5 reports/month |
| Pro | NGOs, consultants | $29-49/month | Unlimited reports, verification, AI copilot |
| Advanced | Corporates, large NGOs | $99-199/month | ESG module, carbon tracking, donor exports |
| Enterprise | Governments, DFIs | Custom | Multi-tenant, API access, dedicated support |

### Additional Revenue Streams
1. **Certification Services** — Bronze ($50) → Silver ($150) → Gold ($300) → Platinum ($500)
2. **Verification-as-a-Service** — Per verified project ($25-100)
3. **Carbon Market Integration** — % fee on carbon credit transactions (future)
4. **Premium AI** — Advanced AI analysis beyond plan limits
5. **Data/API Access** — Aggregate anonymized data for researchers/DFIs
6. **Training/Consulting** — ESG/SDG reporting training programs
