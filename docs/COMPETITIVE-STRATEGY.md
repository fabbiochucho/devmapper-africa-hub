## Go-to-Market (Africa)

### Why We Win
- Fragmented regulatory systems across 54 countries
- Weak verification infrastructure
- High donor dependency
- Rapid ESG adoption

### Entry Strategy
- **Start with:** Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa
- **Focus on:** NGOs + Donor reporting, Corporate ESG, Government pilots
- **Sell:** "Verified reporting", "Audit-ready ESG", "Carbon-ready projects"

### Revenue Streams
1. **SaaS Subscriptions** — Lite, Pro, Advanced, Enterprise
2. **Certification Services** — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum
3. **Verification-as-a-Service** — Per verified project
4. **Carbon Market Integration** (future) — % fee on carbon credits
5. **Premium AI + Verified Reporting** — Audit-grade reports pricing
